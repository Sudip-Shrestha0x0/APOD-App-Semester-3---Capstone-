üåü Our Mission

Stellar View provides an intuitive and beautiful way to explore NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) archive. Our mission is to make cosmic discoveries accessible to everyone, from astronomy enthusiasts to curious minds seeking to understand our universe.

üî≠ What is APOD?

The Astronomy Picture of the Day is one of the most popular websites at NASA. Each day, a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer. The archive dates back to 1995 and contains thousands of spectacular images.
